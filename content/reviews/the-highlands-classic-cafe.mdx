This was a very good Parmi. Good amount of salad. The chips had this great herby salt and a good crunch. 

Parmi had a lovely crunch, good size and nice amount of cheese.

Controversially they did not add and ham or bacon, and missing sauce. 

With a few improvements this could have been a great parmi.

I know this one was missing a few things but they did everything else superbly
