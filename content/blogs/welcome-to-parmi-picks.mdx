# Welcome to Parmi Picks!

G'day and welcome to Parmi Picks, your ultimate guide to finding the best chicken parmigiana in town! We're excited to launch this blog where we'll share our parmi adventures, cooking tips, and everything you need to know about this beloved Aussie pub classic.

## What to Expect

On this blog, you'll find:
- In-depth reviews of chicken parmis from various venues
- Tips for making the perfect parmi at home
- Interviews with chefs and pub owners
- Community recommendations and discussions
