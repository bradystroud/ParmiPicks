The parmi was decent overall. The chicken was nicely deep-fried with a crispy coating, and the salad was fresh with juicy tomatoes. However, the ham was a little cold, and the chips weren’t very crispy, which let the meal down slightly. Overall, an alright parmi with a few small improvements needed.
